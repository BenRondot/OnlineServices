Welcome to State Life Online Services

Welcome to the Care Solutions suite of asset-based long-term care insurance products offered by The State Life Insurance Company, a OneAmerica company. We are proud to offer this portfolio of solutions: Asset-Care®, Annuity Care®, Annuity Care® II, ImmediateCare® and Legacy Care®, all designed to provide policyholders with quality coverage and value. These options have allowed producers to provide a “win-win” solution to thousands of insureds and their families for over 20 years.

To assist you, State Life has an outstanding staff of wholesalers who have many combined years of expertise. They are available to discuss sales ideas and strategies, provide product assistance, conduct CE or client seminars and offer market information.

Contact us at 1-800-275-5101 to reach your state's sales team or for further information and assistance.

Note: Please review the state availability list for products in your state. If the State Life Care Solutions portfolio is available in your state, be sure you have completed appointment forms to that you may begin selling right away.
